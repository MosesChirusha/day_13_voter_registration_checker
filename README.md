---

## Problem
As elections approach, many citizens are unsure whether they are registered to
vote, what documents are required, or where to confirm their status. This
uncertainty can prevent eligible voters from participating.

---

## Solution
A simple browser-based voter registration information portal that:
- Explains voter eligibility requirements
- Provides a readiness checklist
- Shares official steps to verify registration status
- Highlights key election dates
- Works offline for information access

This tool does **not** access or verify voter records.

## Country Context
This implementation uses **Uganda** as an example, with elections scheduled for
the 15th. All information is presented as guidance and must be confirmed through
official Electoral Commission sources.
