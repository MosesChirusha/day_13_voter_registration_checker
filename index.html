Voter Registration Info – Uganda

Election Reminder

The upcoming elections are scheduled for the 15th. Make sure your voter registration is confirmed ahead of time.

Who Can Register to Vote

  • Ugandan citizen
  • 18 years or older
  • Possession of a valid National ID or NIN

Registration Readiness Checklist

How to Check Your Official Status

  • Visit your nearest Electoral Commission office
  • Check voter lists displayed at polling stations
  • Use official Electoral Commission communication channels
This tool does not access voter databases. It helps you prepare and understand where to confirm your status officially.
Always rely on official Electoral Commission sources for final confirmation.
